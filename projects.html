Charlie Welland: Copper Martinis
530 West 27th Street
Los Angeles, CA 90007

The glass rack is made entirely out of 0.5 inch copper piping, carefully shaped to create a sleek and minimalist design that maximizes functionality while remaining visually appealing. The piping is meticulously sanded to a brushed copper look, enhancing the natural beauty and warmth of the copper.
